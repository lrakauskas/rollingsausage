This Privacy Policy applies to the Magic Square mobile application published by Rolling Sausage.

Data Collection

Magic Square itself does not collect, store, or transmit personal information directly.

However, the app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. As part of providing ads, Google may automatically collect certain information from your device.

This may include information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Approximate location
  • App interaction data related to advertising

This information is collected and processed by Google in order to provide and improve advertisements shown in the app.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:
https://developers.google.com/admob/android/play-data-disclosure

Local Data

Any gameplay-related data (such as progress or settings) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

The developer of Magic Square does not sell, share, or transfer user data to any third parties. Any data collected for advertising purposes is handled directly by Google.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising content is provided by Google and subject to Google's policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
